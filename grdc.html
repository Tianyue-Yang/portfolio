This is a semester-long project with the Grandmont Rosedale Development Corporation (GRDC) during the study of contextual inquiry course. We as team Interwalla conducted in-depth interviews and analyzed the interviews notes through making an affinity notes wall.

- Project Overview

Role: UX Researcher

Project Duration: Sep. 2019 - Dec. 2019

Skills: Problem Solving, Communication, Interviews, Researching, Brainstorming

Tools: Microsoft Word, Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Drive

Team Members: Matthew Garvin, Tianyue Yang(Maggie), Joanne Kim, Marcus Thomas

- My Role

As a junior UX researcher, I engaged in every step that is vital for the whole UX consulting process. Along with my team, I have conducted in-depth interviews (either as interviewer or notetaker) with the members of GRDC Vacant Property Task Force, researched on background of the project, vacant property issues in general, competitive analysis, knowledge about information retrieval system and designing for older people, etc. Besides, I have also actively participated in the client meetings, group meetings, brainstorming, and all the other endeavors for achieving regular deliverable reporting.
